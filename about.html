About

Steve currently holds the position of Co-Founder and Chief Product & Technology Officer at Bleach Cyber, a Software as a Service (SaaS) cybersecurity company based in London. In this capacity, Steve leads the product strategy and development with a focus on Business-to-Business (B2B) solutions, leveraging his background in product management and cybersecurity to bring a unique blend of skills to the table. He possesses a strong understanding of product development and management, including DevOps, problem-solving, change management, growth strategies, and feature-driven development. His expertise in business resilience and risk management allows him to effectively mitigate risks and ensure business continuity.

Prior to his current role, Steve served as a Director at Cyvatar, where he demonstrated leadership in driving growth initiatives and managing product management and security services. He led product-led growth initiatives, identified growth strategies, and facilitated collaboration between internal stakeholders and third-party development organizations. Additionally, as Director of Security Services, he provided consultancy services for a subscription-based cybersecurity company, showcasing expertise in security, information security, management, and business strategy.

Steve's previous experience at Fusion Risk Management included serving as a Customer Solutions Lead, where he partnered with customers to address their unique needs, focusing on product management, DevOps, problem-solving, and change management. He provided technical solutions and best practice guidance on risk management and business continuity.

Before transitioning to the private sector, Steve spent 12 years in various roles in the British Army, where he developed leadership, training, and operational expertise. He excelled in high-pressure and fast-paced environments, managing teams, overseeing training programs, and providing expert instruction and mentorship.

Overall, Steve's diverse background and extensive experience make him well-equipped to lead product strategy, development, and cybersecurity initiatives, with a strong focus on innovation, risk management, and customer satisfaction.
